		<p>ELIIT offers participants the opportunity to learn from and alongside internationally renowned clinician researchers who constitute the editorial board and reviewer consortium of the Journal of Medical Imaging and Radiation Sciences from all disciplines. The academy will teach the basics of research on a practical level, from the novice perspective, highlighting all the stages from fruition of an idea to publication of their work. Participants will receive Category “A” credits.</p>
